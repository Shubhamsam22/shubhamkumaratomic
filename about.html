Meet James Clear

James Clear is an American author, speaker, and productivity expert. He is best known for his research and writing on habit formation, decision-making, and continuous improvement.

Before becoming a writer, James faced personal setbacks, including a serious injury in high school. Through discipline and small, intentional habits, he rebuilt his life—an experience that later inspired his book Atomic Habits, published in 2018.

Today, James Clear’s work has been translated into over 50 languages and has sold millions of copies worldwide. His popular newsletter and blog reach over 1 million subscribers. He is passionate about helping people harness the power of tiny habits to achieve big goals.
